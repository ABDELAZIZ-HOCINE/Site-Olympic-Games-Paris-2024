Welcome to the Official Website of Paris 2024 Olympic Games!

The Paris 2024 Olympic Games promise to be an extraordinary event, bringing together athletes from around the globe in the City of Light. With a rich Olympic tradition and a deep-seated passion for sport ingrained in our culture, Paris is ready to welcome the world to celebrate unity, excellence, and friendship. Whether it's swimming in the Seine, running along the Champs-Élysées, or climbing to the top of the Eiffel Tower, the 2024 Games will be an unforgettable celebration of human strength, determination, and camaraderie. Join us as we prepare to write the next chapter of Olympic history, right here in Paris, in 2024.
